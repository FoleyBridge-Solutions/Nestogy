# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about using Nestogy ERP for your MSP business.

## General Questions

### What is Nestogy?

Nestogy is a comprehensive ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system designed specifically for Managed Service Providers (MSPs). It combines ticketing, time tracking, invoicing, contract management, asset tracking, and more into one integrated platform.

### Who is Nestogy for?

Nestogy is built for MSPs of all sizes - from solo technicians to large IT service providers. If you provide managed IT services to clients, Nestogy can help you streamline operations and grow your business.

### How is Nestogy different from other PSA tools?

Nestogy is built specifically for modern MSPs with a focus on usability, integration, and automation. Unlike legacy PSA tools, Nestogy offers a modern interface, real-time updates with Livewire, and seamless integration with popular RMM and accounting platforms.

### Is Nestogy cloud-based or self-hosted?

Nestogy can be deployed both ways. We offer a fully managed cloud solution (SaaS) or self-hosted deployment for organizations that require on-premise hosting.

### What browsers does Nestogy support?

Nestogy works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.

## Tickets & Support

### How do I create a ticket?

Click "New Ticket" in the quick actions or navigate to Tickets → Create Ticket. Select a client, enter details, and assign to a technician.

### Can clients submit tickets directly?

Yes! Through the client portal, your customers can submit tickets 24/7, which automatically appear in your ticket queue.

### Can we convert emails to tickets?

Absolutely. Configure your support email address in Settings → Email, and incoming emails will automatically create tickets.

### How do SLAs work in Nestogy?

SLAs are defined in contracts and automatically applied to tickets based on priority. Nestogy tracks response and resolution times, providing alerts when SLAs are at risk.

### Can we customize ticket statuses and priorities?

Yes. You can fully customize ticket statuses, priorities, categories, and workflows in Settings → Tickets.

## Time Tracking

### How do I track time on tickets?

Open any ticket and click "Start Timer" to begin tracking. Click "Stop" when done. You can also manually enter time after the work is completed.

### Can time be tracked on mobile devices?

Yes. The Nestogy mobile app includes full time tracking functionality, including timers and manual time entry.

### How does billable vs. non-billable time work?

When logging time, you can mark it as billable (will be invoiced to client) or non-billable (internal time). Default billing status can be set per activity type.

### Can we track breaks?

Yes. Nestogy supports break tracking and can automatically deduct breaks from time entries based on your configured rules.

### How do we handle overtime?

Configure overtime rules in Settings → Time Tracking. Nestogy will automatically calculate overtime based on daily or weekly thresholds and apply appropriate rates.

## Invoicing & Billing

### How do I create an invoice?

Navigate to Financial → Invoices → Create Invoice. Select a client, add line items (services, time entries, products), and send to the client.

### Can we accept online payments?

Yes! Nestogy integrates with Stripe, PayPal, and ACH for online payment processing. Clients can pay invoices directly through the portal or email.

### How do we handle recurring invoices?

Create a standard invoice, then click "Make Recurring". Configure the frequency and Nestogy will automatically generate and send invoices on schedule.

### Can we import time entries into invoices?

Yes. When creating an invoice, click "Add Time Entries" to select billable time from a date range and automatically convert them to line items.

### How are taxes calculated?

Configure tax rates in Settings → Financial. Taxes can be applied automatically or manually controlled per line item. Nestogy supports both tax-inclusive and tax-exclusive pricing.

## Contracts & SLAs

### What types of contracts does Nestogy support?

Nestogy supports various contract types including managed services agreements, block hours, project-based contracts, and per-device/per-user pricing models.

### How do bucket hours work?

Bucket hour contracts include a pre-purchased number of hours each month. As you log time to tickets, hours are deducted from the bucket. Nestogy tracks usage and alerts you when running low.

### Can contracts auto-renew?

Yes. Configure auto-renewal settings when creating a contract. Nestogy will automatically extend the contract and notify the client according to your settings.

### How are SLAs enforced?

SLAs defined in contracts are automatically applied to tickets. Nestogy tracks compliance in real-time and provides visual indicators and alerts when SLAs are at risk or breached.

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within minutes.

### Why can't I see a specific client/ticket?

Check your permissions and filters. Some data may be restricted based on your user role. Contact your administrator if you believe you should have access.

### The timer stopped unexpectedly. What happened?

Timers automatically stop after periods of inactivity to prevent accidental time logging. Check your time entries and manually adjust if needed.

### Why didn't my client receive an email?

Check spam/junk folders, verify the email address is correct, and ensure email notifications are enabled in Settings → Email.

### How do I report a bug?

Email support@nestogy.com with a detailed description of the issue, including steps to reproduce, screenshots, and your browser/device information.
